# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior for a retail business to uncover purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and factors influencing sales performance. The analysis combines Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform raw data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand customer purchasing behavior across demographics, product categories, sales channels, and seasonal trends.

### Objective

Answer the business question:

> How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains customer shopping transactions and includes information such as:

- Customer demographics
- Product categories
- Purchase amounts
- Shopping frequency
- Discounts applied
- Payment methods
- Customer reviews
- Seasonal purchases
- Online and offline sales channels
- Subscription status
